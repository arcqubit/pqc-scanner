## 🎨 Brand Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Authoritative** but approachable
- **Technical** but not jargon-heavy
- **Urgent** but not alarmist
- **Innovative** but practical

### Messaging Do's
✅ Emphasize automation and speed
✅ Use concrete numbers and stats
✅ Show before/after comparisons
✅ Focus on developer experience
✅ Highlight compliance benefits

### Messaging Don'ts
❌ Overhype the quantum threat (be factual)
❌ Dismiss competitors (focus on our strengths)
❌ Promise 100% security (realistic claims)
❌ Use excessive technical jargon
❌ Neglect the "why now" urgency
